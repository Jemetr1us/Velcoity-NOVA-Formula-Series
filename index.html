Velocity NOVA Formula Series is a premier motorsport organisation dedicated to discovering, developing, and showcasing the next generation of world-class drivers. VNFS operates four championships — from Formula 3 through to our flagship F1, plus a full Endurance ladder spanning GT3, LMP2, and Hypercar.

Founded in 2026 on the belief that motorsport should be accessible, competitive, and spectacular, we provide the infrastructure, technical excellence, and competitive environment that turns raw talent into champions.
